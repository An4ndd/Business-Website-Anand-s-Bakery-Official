Anand's Story


Anand Mistry | Founder & Owner




My passion for baking started during the COVID-19 pandemic. I started trying out new recipes and felt excited to share my food with others. I opened my bakery in 2020 and have been perfecting my recipes ever since, always looking to innovate and improve.



Our Ingredients


Our commitment to | fresh & locally sourced ingredients




We promise to only use the freshest and locally sourced ingredients so you can 'Eat well, feel well'! We believe that making great food starts with the proper ingredients, grown in the proper places, by the proper people.
